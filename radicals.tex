\begin{description}
\item[Degree $1$] Given the equation $ax+b=0$ with $a>0$,\footnote{Here
and in other cases we can assume $a\not<0$ by dividing the equation by
$-1$.} divide each side by $a$ to get $x+\frac ba=0$.\\
Next subtract $-\frac ba$ from each side to finally get $x=\linear ab$.
\item[Degree $2$] Given the equation $ax^2+bx+c=0$ with $a>0$, divide each
side by $a$ to get $x^2+ \frac bax+\frac ca=0$.\\
Next complete the square to get $\left(x+\frac b{2a}\right)^2+
\frac ca=\frac{b^2}{4a^2}$. We need to assume $2\neq 0$.\\
Now subtract $\frac ca$ from each 
side and put the right hand side on a common denominator to get 
$\left(x+\frac b{2a}\right)^2=\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}$.\\
Take square roots on each side to get $x+\frac b{2a}=\pm\frac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}
{2a}$. Now subtract $  \frac b{2a}$ from each side to get 
$x=\quadratic abc$.
\item[Degree $3$] Given the equation $ax^3+bx^2+cx+d=0$ with 
$a>0$, divide each side by $a$ to get $x^3+ \frac bax^2+\frac 
cax+\frac da=0$.\\
Next complete the cube to get $(x+\frac b{3a})^3
+\frac cax+\frac da=\frac{b^2}{3a^2}x+\frac{b^3}{27a^3}$, that is 
to say 
$(x+\frac b{3a})^3+\frac
{3ac-b^2}{3a^2}(x+\frac b{3a})+\frac{27a^2d+2b^3-9abc}{27a^3}=0$.\\
We need to assume $3\neq 0$.
Write $x+\frac b{3a}=u+v$, with $uv=\frac {b^2-3ac}{9a^2}$ in 
order to obtain $u^3+3uv(u+v)+v^3-3uv(u+v)+\frac{27a^2d+2b^3-9abc}
{27a^3}=0$.\\
That is, $u^3+v^3+\frac{27a^2d+2b^3-9abc}{27a^3}=0$, or, on multiplying by $u^3$ and using $uv=\frac {b^2-3ac}{9a^2},\ 
u^6+\frac{27a^2d+2b^3-9abc}{27a^3}u^3+\left(\frac {b^2-3ac}{9a^2}
\right)^3=0$.\\
This enables us to obtain $u^3=\frac{\frac{9abc-27
a^2d-2b^3}{27a^3}\pm\sqrt{\left(\frac{9abc-27a^2d-2b^3}{27a^3}
\right)^2+4\left(\frac{3ac-b^2}{9a^2}\right)^3}}2$, assuming $2\neq 0$.\\
We thus 
obtain $u=\frac{\sqrt[3]{\frac{9abc-27a^2d-2b^3+\sqrt{(9abc-27a^2d
-2b^3)^2+4(3ac-b^2)^3}}2}}{3a},\,v=\frac{\sqrt[3]{\frac{9abc-27a^2
d-2b^3-\sqrt{(9abc-27a^2d-2b^3)^2+4(3ac-b^2)^3}}2}}{3a}$.\\ 
For each of the three solutions to $u$, we take for $v$ the corresponding
solution such that their product is $\frac {b^2-3ac}{9a^2}$. Then we obtain\\
$x=\cubic[a]{b}{c}{d}$,  where the three possible cube roots for\\
$u$ give three solutions for $x$.
\item[Degree $4$] Given the equation $ax^4+bx^3+cx^2+dx+e=0$ with 
$a\neq 0$, divide each side by $a$ to get\\
$x^4+ \frac bax^3+\frac cax^2+\frac dax+\frac ea=0$. Now complete the
tesseract to get 
$\left(x+\frac b{4a}\right)^4+\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2}x^2+\frac{16a^2
d-b^3}{16a^3}x+\frac{256a^3e-b^4}{256a^4}=0$, or\\
$\left(x+\frac b{4a}\right)^4+\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2}\left(x+\frac b{4a}\right)^2+
\frac{16a^2d+2b^3-8abc}{16a^3}\left(x+\frac b{4a}\right)+
\frac{256a^3e-3b^4+16ab^2c-64a^2bd}{256a^4}=0$. Writing $y=x+
\frac b{4a}$, this reduces to\\
$y^4+\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2}y^2+
\frac{8a^2d+b^3-4abc}{8a^3}y+\frac{256a^3e-3b^4+16ab^2c-64a^2bd}
{256a^4}=0$. We had to assume $2\neq 0$.
If $8a^2d+b^3-4abc=0$, then $x=\left\{\begin{array}{l}-\frac
b{4a}\pm\sqrt{\frac{\frac{3b^2-8ac}{8a^2}+\sqrt{\left(\frac{3b^2-8ac}{8a^2}
\right)^2-4\left(\frac{256a^3e-3b^4+16ab^2c-64a^2bd}{256a^4}
\right)}}2},\cr-\frac b{4a}\pm\sqrt{\frac{\frac{3b^2-8ac}{8a^2}-
\sqrt{\left(\frac{3b^2-8ac}{8a^2}\right)^2-4\left(\frac{256a^3e-
3b^4+16ab^2c-64a^2bd}{256a^4}\right)}}2}.\end{array}\right.$\\
If not, we now factor this to get $(y^2+Dy+E)(y^2-Dy+F)=0$, where
$E+F-D^2=\frac{8ac-3b^2}{8a^2},\,
(F-E)D=\frac{8a^2d+b^3-4abc}{8a^3},\\
EF=\frac{256a^3e-3b^4+16ab^2c-64a^2bd}{256a^4}$. It is immediately obvious that\\
$64a^6D^6+(128a^5c-48a^4b^2)D^4
+(64a^4c^2+64a^4bd+12a^2b^4-64a^3b^2c-256a^5e)D^2\\
{}\qquad+(64a^4bcd+8ab^4c-64a^4d^2-b^6-16a^2b^2c^2-16a^2b^3d)=0$, a cubic
in
$D^2$. 
Thus we have, assuming $3\neq 0$,\\
$D^2=
\frac{
3b^2-8ac+4a\sqrt[3]{
\frac{27(ad^2+b^2e)+2c^3-9c(8ae+
(3a-2)bd)+\sqrt{(27(ad^2+b^2e)+2c^3-9c(8ae+(3a-2)bd))^2+4(3bd-c^2-12ae)^3}}2}}
{12a^2}\\
{}\qquad\qquad+\frac{4a\sqrt[3]{\frac{27(ad^2+b^2e)+2c^3-9c(8ae+(3a-2)bd)
-\sqrt{(27(ad^2+b^2e)+2c^3-9c(8ae+(3a-2)bd))^2+4(3bd-c^2-12ae)^3}}2}}{12a^2}$.\\
